I am Claudio, an AI and data science engineer focused on systems that make complex information easier to search, reason over, and reuse.

I currently work as a Data Scientist at Avvale, where I am building an AI Offer Advisor. The project combines semantic search, document ingestion, and generative AI to help teams find relevant historical offers and produce new proposal material from them.

My research and project work is mostly around language models, recommendation data, agent architectures, and applied deep learning. I have worked on automatic prompt optimization with GEPA, a 2APL multi-agent system, and a DeepQueueNet implementation for traffic-delay prediction on large packet-simulation datasets.

I studied Computer Engineering at the Polytechnic of Bari and continued with a master's in AI and Data Science. I also spent an Erasmus period at the Polytechnic of Barcelona, where I worked with telecommunication and applied AI problems.
